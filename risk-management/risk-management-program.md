# ⚠️ Risk Management Program

## Overview

XYZ Academy applies a structured risk management approach to identify, evaluate, and reduce cybersecurity risks affecting academic systems, organizational operations, and institutional data.

The risk management process focuses on protecting critical assets while supporting operational continuity and organizational resilience.

---

## Risk Management Objectives

The program aims to:

- Identify critical organizational assets
- Evaluate potential cybersecurity threats
- Assess operational impact
- Reduce vulnerability exposure
- Support incident readiness
- Improve organizational resilience

---

## Asset Classification

Organizational assets were categorized based on operational importance and business impact.

| Asset Category | Examples | Criticality |
|---|---|---|
| Academic Data | Student records, grades, attendance | High |
| Learning Systems | LMS platform, academic portals | High |
| Communication Services | Email systems, staff communication | Medium |
| End-User Devices | Staff laptops, student workstations | Medium |
| Cloud Services | Cloud backup platforms | High |
| Network Infrastructure | Routers, switches, wireless access points | High |

---

## Risk Assessment Criteria

Risks were evaluated using multiple factors:

| Factor | Description |
|---|---|
| Threat Likelihood | Probability of threat occurrence |
| Business Impact | Potential operational damage |
| Asset Criticality | Importance of affected systems |
| Vulnerability Exposure | Weaknesses exploitable by attackers |

Risk levels were categorized as:

- Low
- Medium
- High
- Critical

---

## High-Priority Risks

| Risk | Potential Impact | Mitigation Strategy |
|---|---|---|
| Phishing Attacks | Credential compromise | MFA + awareness training |
| Ransomware | Loss of academic services | Offline backups + endpoint protection |
| Weak Passwords | Unauthorized access | Strong password policy |
| Insider Misuse | Exposure of sensitive information | RBAC enforcement |
| Malware Infections | Device compromise | Antivirus & monitoring controls |
| Service Outages | LMS disruption | Backup recovery procedures |

---

## Risk Mitigation Strategy

The organization applies layered security controls to reduce operational risks.

### Implemented Controls

- Security awareness training
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA)
- Access control enforcement
- Endpoint protection
- Backup & recovery procedures
- Continuous monitoring
- Incident escalation workflows

These controls improve operational resilience while supporting secure academic operations.

---

## Organizational Impact

Effective risk management improves the protection of academic systems, reduces operational disruption, and supports the confidentiality, integrity, and availability (CIA) of institutional information assets.
